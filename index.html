Ankita Rajput, Director
(Operations and Finance)

Ankita Rajput is the Director of Operations & Finance at Ketak Communications.Under Ankita's leadership, Ketak Communications is making life easier for brands and building trust with customers. It is investing heavily in Marketing, Advertising, and acquiring creative talent around the globe. ...

Ankita has been part of many Social service groups from her college days, and in implementing strategies for its associates and the company's culture. She became Co-founder & Director of Operations & Finance at Ketak Communications in 2023. From 2018 to 2019 she worked in XDBS as a part of the Lead generation department, and from 2020 to 2022 she served in Capita India for several departments such as Payment management, Order management & Logistics.

Ankita is originally from Nashik, Maharashtra. She graduated from Pune University with a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and completed a master’s degree in business administration from Liverpool University, United Kingdom.
